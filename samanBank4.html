Left-Handed People's Day - #2

The Left-Handed People's Day gift card design showcases a blue circle with an illustration of a left hand presenting a credit card. A small text accompanies the design, saying "my lefty, your day be blessed." The design celebrates left-handed individuals, promoting inclusivity and offering well wishes for the occasion.

Book Reading Day

The design represents the celebration of reading, with pink symbolizing warmth and creativity. The blue accents add an elegant touch, capturing the enchantment of books. This design inspires individuals to immerse themselves in the captivating world of literature on Book Reading Day.

Graduation Celebration - #2

The Graduation Day gift card design showcases an illustration of graduation caps being thrown in the air against a light blue background. This design serves as a visual representation of the memorable milestone of graduation, celebrating the hard work and dedication of individuals as they embark on new paths.
