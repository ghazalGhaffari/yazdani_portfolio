Tabarestan Plastic Complex

Project: catalog

I had the honor of collaborating with the esteemed TABARESTAN PLASTIC COMPLEX, a longstanding regional brand dedicated to producing water tanks. In addition to showcasing their high-quality products and their specifications in a comprehensive catalog, I highlighted their noble mission of bringing clean, drinkable water to underserved communities in Iran. By featuring their impactful campaigns, we aimed to raise awareness about their admirable efforts to improve lives in impoverished areas.
