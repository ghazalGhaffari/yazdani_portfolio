Occasion-Specific Gift Credit Card Designs

Father's Day

The Father's Day gift card design features an old cassette tape with a handwritten message in Persian that translates to "for my father, the song of my life." The design pays tribute to the profound impact fathers have on their children's lives, expressing heartfelt gratitude and love.

Engineers Day

The gift card design for Engineers Day showcases a ruler-like illustration with centimeters as markings, symbolizing precision and measurement. The design incorporates a color scheme of yellow and white, representing clarity and accuracy.

Congratulations on Buying a New House

The design draws inspiration from the popular “Brick Game”, showcasing colorful bricks forming a house structure. The vibrant colors and interactive element of the brick game evoke a sense of anticipation and happiness, making it a fitting design to congratulate someone on their new home.
