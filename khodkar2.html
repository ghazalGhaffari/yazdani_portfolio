Identity Concepts

PEN

The primary symbol used in the logo is a pen shape, which directly corresponds to the company's name, "Khodkar," meaning "pen" in Farsi. This element serves as a focal point, emphasizing the company's core product and expertise in the stationary industry.

mountain goat, a traditional Iranian motif

To incorporate a touch of Iranian heritage, an image of a mountain goat, a traditional Iranian motif, is included in the logo design. This element adds a unique cultural aspect, highlighting the company's local roots and celebrating the rich traditions of Iran.

communication

In addition to the pen shape, a communication symbol is integrated into the logo, representing Khodkar's commitment to effective communication and customer engagement. This symbol underscores the company's dedication to providing exceptional service and building strong relationships with clients.

upward arrow

Furthermore, an upward arrow shape is employed to signify progress and growth. This symbol conveys Khodkar's aspiration for continuous improvement, innovation, and advancement in the stationary industry. It represents the company's forward-thinking approach and its commitment to staying ahead of the curve.
